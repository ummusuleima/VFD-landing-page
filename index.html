SoftVFD - EFD Digital Solution

An online solution for business owners to issue TRA Receipts without the need of an EFD Machine.

Get started See how it works

About SoftVFD

SoftVFD is a system which enable businesses to issue digital fiscal receipts without the need of an EFD Machine. With SoftVFD one can register products , clients , shops , input sales, share digital fiscal receipt with customer via email or Whatsapp, view different reports and dashboard etc.

Why SoftVFD ?

SoftVFD aims at eliminating the use of EFD machine through provision of a well intergrated portal that will be easy to use and more cost effective but still functioning as an EFD Machine.This will reduce the cost of maintaining the Machine once damaged.

Efficient

Eliminates the need for physical checks and delay for a service due to network connection thus saves time for both the Tax Administrator and the taxpayers hence smooth provision and getting the services.

Cloud Based Solution

The solution will be hosted on high perfomance servers for best online experience.softVFD system can be accessed all over once needed.

Transparent

Transparency occurs through Sendig existing real-time data to the Tax Administrator and ensures continuous monitoring of taxpayers.

    • How to use SoftVFD?

    SoftVFD Web Portal

    Keep track of your customers, products, all your sales transactions and print receipts via VFD online portal using your account credentials.

    SoftVFD Mobile Application

    Through softVFD Mobile Application sales persons will be able to interact with the system through given credentials thus can record all their sales transactions, register their customer, and print receipts and be able to share the receipts through email or whatsapp.

    SoftVFD API's

    This is through Integrating your softVFD account and make your all activities such as sales transactions,client registration,product registration and print receipts direct from your existing systems.

    Questions? look here!

    The following are fluently asked question and their posible answers

    What is softVFD?

    is a new system that aims at eliminate high dependancy of EFD instead use of VFD that is directly linked to the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) in associate with Softnet Technologies Company who acts as a Middleman for the system that help the taxpayer to issue valid VFD receipts using smart phones or computers.

    Do i need an EFD Machine to use softVFD?

    Not at all you dont need to have an EFD machine to access the VFD application since VFD is an online system that can be accessed through a web portal as well as a mobile application where with specific credential a user can use to access the system so there is no need of EFD machine.Infact the aim of VFD is to eliminate EFD there for no reason for it to be used again.

    What are the requirements to get VFD account?

    To get VFD account one is required to have TIN Certificates,Business Licence,copy of ID, customer information form and VRN Certificate (if VAT Registered). For more details please contact us

    Can SoftVFD system intergrate with third part application?

    Sure,softVFD application architecture allows integrating it with other applications through its secure API's.
